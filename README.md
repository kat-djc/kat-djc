## Hey there, I'm Kat 👋

I'm a data scientist, writer, and civic data transparency activist based in Chicago :)

I currently cover local government meetings as part of City Bureau's Documenters Network and preserve vulnerable datasets as a volunteer developer for the Data Liberation Project. In 2025, I was selected as a Gary Marx Fellow by Investigative Reporters & Editors, a fellowship recognizing promising early-career journalists.

I most recently worked as a Data Scientist at Warner Bros. Discovery, working on a wide variety of applied machine learning projects across Streaming, Studio Engineering, Finance, and Human Resources. I graduated in 2020 with a B.A. in Data Science from the University of California, Berkeley.
